Share Point migration

We can help in migrating to SharePoint 2013 from an earlier version or from a different content management system, from soups to nuts of managing everything from backend deployment to data migration and end user training. Microsoft has made several important improvements with SharePoint 2013. Upgrading to this version gives you access to:

  • Dramatically Improved searching
  • Better mobile access and non-IE browser support
  • Improved user interface and My Sites
  • More robust business intelligence capabilities

On Cloud & On-Premise Implementation

One of the first decisions to make with SharePoint is whether to host the site on-premise or use a cloud-based SharePoint solution like Office 365. Office 365 offers a stable, turn-key solution that lets us focus on development and you on your business. On-Premise servers offer more flexibility and a lower maintenance cost. We have extensive experience with both options to work with you to help decide which option best fits your budget and requirements.
